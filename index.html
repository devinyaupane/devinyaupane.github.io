Why Finance?
I started in accounting because I wanted to understand how businesses work from the inside. Then I realized there was a whole analytical layer — the "why behind the numbers" — that I hadn't touched. That curiosity is what drove me first to analytics and then to SCU.
Why Analytics?
At Insync, I saw Python and AI changing how financial analysis gets done. I could either watch it happen or be part of it. I chose to be part of it. The MSFA was deliberate — I wanted to be the analyst who bridges both worlds, not just one.
What Drives Me
Curiosity. The need to explore and understand deeply. I'm not satisfied with just running the numbers — I want to know what they mean, what story they tell, and what decision they should drive. That's why I'm building portfolio projects, not just updating a resume.
What I'm Looking For
A team that values both financial rigor and analytical thinking. A place where I can build models AND build dashboards. FP&A, Financial Analyst, or Data Analyst roles where I can grow into a senior analytical leadership position over the next 3–5 years.
